What problem did they face?

SuessCo faced critical challenges in managing and monitoring their industrial operations across multiple sites. They lacked real-time visibility into equipment performance, leading to costly downtimes, inefficient maintenance scheduling, and missed opportunities for optimization.

Manual data collection and fragmented systems created delays in decision-making, while growing demand for regulatory compliance and environmental impact tracking further complicated their operational landscape.
